Exotica is a 1994 Canadian film set primarily in and around the Exotica strip club in Toronto, Canada. It was written and directed by Atom Egoyan. Music used includes "Montagues and Capulets". Exotica presents a disparate group of characters whose lives are interconnected through the Exotica nightclub.
