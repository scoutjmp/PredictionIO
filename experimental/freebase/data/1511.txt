Children of the Revolution (1996)
/m/0fm079
Children of the Revolution is a 1996 Australian historic comedy film, depicting Joseph Stalin and his son's somewhat deterministic path into The Revolution in modern day Australia. The film stars Judy Davis, Geoffrey Rush, Sam Neill, and F. Murray Abraham as Joseph Stalin. Joseph Stalin (F. Murray Abraham) spent his last night in the arms of the Australian Joan (Judy Davis). The story describes how their "love-child" brought Australia to the brink of civil war. Children of the Revolution grossed $969,444 at the box office in Australia.
