/m/0b77v3j
A Smile Like Yours is a 1997 romantic comedy film directed by Keith Samples and starring Greg Kinnear and Lauren Holly. The film centers on a couple as they try to conceive a child. The film was produced by Rysher Entertainment and released by Paramount Pictures. The title song was performed by Natalie Cole. Holly's performance in the film earned her a Razzie Award nomination for Worst Actress (also for Turbulence).
