The Substitute 4: Failure Is Not An Option is a 2001 action film. Starring Treat Williams once again as Karl Thomasson who is now an undercover policeman that must infiltrate a military school's faculty to cease the actions of a white supremacist cult. This was the last entry in the popular "The Substitute" series and was released direct-to-video. Karl Thommasson(Willaims), ex-Special Forces soldier and retired mercenary, is now a working as a police detective. One day, he is approached by old army buddy Teague who gives him a mission: working udercover at a military school where Ted, Teague's nephew, is one of the cadets. Teague believes that a white supremacist cult is being run at the school and its members are the cadets and the school faculty. Trent accepts the mission and begins working as a history teacher at the school. While working at the school, Karl teams up with Devlin,a former member of Karl's Mercenary team who works at the school as a martial arts teacher. They learn that Brack is leader the cult and Ted is one of the cult members.
