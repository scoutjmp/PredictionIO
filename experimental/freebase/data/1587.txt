Terror in a Texas Town is a 1958 American Western film, directed by Joseph Lewis. The script was written by Dalton Trumbo, but due to Trumbo's status on the Hollywood Blacklist, Ben Perry initially received screenwriting credit. It stars Sterling Hayden, Sebastian Cabot, and Carol Kelly, with Eugene Mazolla, Nedrick Young, and Victor Millan costarring. Sheb Wooley plays Baxter. A Swedish whaler is out for revenge when he finds out that a greedy oil man murdered his father for their land.
