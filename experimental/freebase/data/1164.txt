Zeus and Roxanne (1997)

Zeus and Roxanne is a 1997 family comedy/adventure film directed by George T. Miller. The film revolves around the friendship between the title characters, a dog and a dolphin, respectively. The film stars Steve Guttenberg and Kathleen Quinlan. The movie was panned.  According to Rotten Tomatoes, 50% of critics gave the film a positive review (out of seven reviews) with an average rating of 4.4/10. The movie debuted at No.10
