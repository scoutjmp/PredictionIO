Entertaining Angels: the Dorothy Day Story is a 1996 independent film about the life of Dorothy Day, the journalist turned social activist and founder of the Catholic Worker newspaper. The film stars Moira Kelly as Day, Heather Graham, Lenny Von Dohlen and Martin Sheen. Kelly and Sheen are also co-stars of the NBC dramatic series The West Wing. Kelly, Von Dohlen and Graham have previously appeared in David Lynch's Twin Peaks: Fire Walk with Me.
