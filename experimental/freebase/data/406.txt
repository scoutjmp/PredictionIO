Billy Halleck (Robert John Burke) is an arrogant, obese upper-class lawyer living in suburban Maine with his daughter Linda (Bethany Joy Lenz) and his wife Heidi (Lucinda Jenney). After defending an underworld crime boss named Richie "The Hammer" Ginelli (Joe Mantegna) in court, the town is in the middle of a carnival which is run by Gypsies. One night, while driving, Billy accidentally runs over an old Gypsy woman with his car when Heidi distracts him by performing oral sex on him. The Gypsy woman's father, 108-year old Tadzu Lempke (Michael Constantine), curses Billy by touching his face and saying the word, "Thinner". The curse causes Billy to rapidly lose weight, regardless of how much he eats.
