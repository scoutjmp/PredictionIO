Chasers (1994)
/m/03m3y1s
Chasers is a 1994 comedy film directed by Dennis Hopper. It is about a pair of United States Navy SPs (Tom Berenger and William McNamara) who must escort a beautiful prisoner (Erika Eleniak), and the troubles they encounter. Gruff Chief Petty Officer Rock Reilly (Tom Berenger), and a young yeoman, Eddie Devane (William McNamara) have to escort a very seductive young woman, Ensign Toni Johnson (Erika Eleniak). She manages to seduce Eddie and escapes to the hands of women's underwear seller Doggie (director Dennis Hopper in a cameo) and has to encounter Marine prison guard, Sgt. Vince Banger (Gary Busey).
