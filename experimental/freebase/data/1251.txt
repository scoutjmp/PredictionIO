A Chef in Love (1996)
/m/026dvsf
A Chef in Love, (Georgian: შეყვარებული კულინარის 1001 რეცეპტი / Shekvarebuli kulinaris ataserti retsepti) is a 1996 Georgian film directed by Nana Dzhordzhadze. It stars Pierre Richard and Nino Kirtadze. It is about a French opera singer and chef living in Georgia who opens a restaurant. It was nominated for an Academy Award for Best Foreign Language Film.
